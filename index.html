Frequently Asked Questions

We hope the below answers any of your queries you may have. However please contact Nam if you have any queries that are not answered below.

I have a dietary requirement, who should I contact?

Please contact Nam to include any dietary requirements at the Reception.

When would you like me to RSVP by?

We would greatly appreciate if you could RSVP by Thursday, 2 April 2020 to confirm final attendees with Sun Kitchen.

Are children welcome?

Most definitely, children are welcome for both the ceremony and reception. For the reception, please let us know during RSVP so that we can request Sun Kitchen to provide space for the pram or an extra chair / high chair to accomodate for them.

Where can I park at St Mary’s Church?

Street parking is available in front of St Mary’s Church on Dandenong Road or on Westbury Street (turn left just before St Mary’s Church if travelling towards the City).

Where can I park at Sun Kitchen?

Ticketed parking is available right outside Sun Kitchen at $5.20 all day. Upon arrival please proceed to the ticket machine with your vehicle registration number to input into the machine (EFTPOS and Coins accepted).

What is the ideal method to commute?

Please consider Car Pooling or utilising a Taxi / Ride Sharing service as there will be plenty of drinks that night to consume.

What drinks will there be following the Ceremony?

There will be Soft Drink, Beer, Wine (White and Red), Japanese Sake and a variety of Spirits (Gin, Whisky, Tequila, etc.) to enjoy, have a great time and drink heaps (but responsibly please).

What type of Chinese cuisine is served at Sun Kitchen?

Sun Kitchen specialises in Cantonese and Sichuan style cuisine, we hope you will enjoy the Chinese Banquet we have selected for the reception.

We would love to bring a gift, do you have a wedding registry?

Thank you for thinking of giving us a gift. We will have a Wishing Well by the registration table, however if you would like to bear us a gift, please donate to Wildlife Victoria that would be greatly appreciated.

We love your invitations, who were they made by?

Our invitations were made by our uncle Hung Luong and his design company Keyzy Graphics. The Luong family has been in the graphic design and print industry for over thirty years. If you need any graphic designs, be sure to hit up Keyzy Graphics.

Will there be a Japan Wedding, if so can we come?

A popular question from Nam's family. Unfortunately or fortunately (for us) there will be no wedding in Japan. However, we are going in late May and you are welcome to be a third or thirteenth wheel.

Will Nam be speaking Vietnamese at the wedding?

Maybe...if he does, apologies in advance.
